Joji

Who is Joji?

Joji, born George Miller, is a singer, songwriter, and producer known for his emotionally raw and atmospheric sound. He transitioned from internet fame to becoming a respected artist, using music as a way to express vulnerability, loneliness, and self-reflection.

His Life & Journey

Joji started his creative career on YouTube, where he gained massive attention for his humor and characters. Eventually, he stepped away from that world to focus entirely on music, choosing authenticity over fame.

His journey shows growth — from chaos and comedy to quiet introspection and emotional honesty.

His Music & Themes

Joji’s music explores themes of love, heartbreak, isolation, and nostalgia. His sound blends lo-fi, R&B, and alternative pop, creating songs that feel personal and late-night introspective.

Albums like Nectar and SMITHEREENS feel like emotional diaries — soft, imperfect, and real.
